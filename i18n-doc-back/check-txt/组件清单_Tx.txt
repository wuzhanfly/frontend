## 需要国际化的组件

TxPendingAlert 组件 - 发现硬编码文本：
- "This transaction is pending confirmation." (交易待确认提示文本)

TxSocketAlert 组件 - 发现硬编码文本：
- "An error has occurred while fetching transaction info. Please click here to update." (交易信息获取错误提示文本)

TxInfo 组件 - 发现硬编码文本：
- "Unique character string (TxID) assigned to every verified transaction" (交易哈希提示文本)
- "Current transaction state: Success, Failed (Error), or Pending (In Process)" (交易状态提示文本)
- "Batch index for this transaction" (批次索引提示文本)
- "L1 Data Fee which is used to cover the L1 'security' cost from the batch submission mechanism. In combination with L2 execution fee, L1 fee makes the total amount of fees that a transaction pays." (L1费用说明文本)
- "A Dynamic overhead (fee scalar) premium, which serves as a buffer in case L1 prices rapidly increase." (L1费用标量说明文本)
- "Amount of gas used by the blob" (Blob Gas用量提示文本)
- "Amount of {currencyUnits.ether} used for blobs in this transaction" (Blob Gas费用提示文本)
- "Binary data included with the transaction. See logs tab for additional info" (原始输入提示文本)
- "Base Fee refers to the network Base Fee at the time of the block, while Max Fee & Max Priority Fee refer to the max amount a user is willing to pay {validator} fee distribution info" (Gas费用分布信息提示文本中部分是硬编码的)
